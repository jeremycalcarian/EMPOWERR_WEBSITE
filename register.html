EMPOWERR 5K

In Partnership with Rooted

Turn every step into impact.

Be Part of Something Bigger

Since 2020, EMPOWERR has empowered youth across communities through fundraising, partnerships, and hands-on service. Now, we're expanding our reach by registering as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit — and this 5K is a major milestone in that journey. Your participation fuels our mission.

Rooted, a newly formed group, is on a mission to spotlight community challenges through app development. Funds raised from this event will help bring their tech solutions to life.

By signing up, you're not just joining a race — you're investing in youth-driven change. Bring a friend. Bring the energy. Make it count.

  • Date: July 16, 2025
  • Location: Hagerstown City Park (in-person) + Virtual Option
  • Includes: T-shirt, bib, refreshments, and prize entries
  • Impact: Supports student-led local projects and app development
