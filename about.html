About EMPOWERR

Our story, our vision, our impact.

Who We Are

EMPOWERR is a student-led initiative dedicated to community empowerment, education, and sustainable development. Since 2020, we've raised significant funds, forged partnerships with leading corporations, and expanded our network to over 35 schools across MD, WV, and PA.

What Makes Us Different

EMPOWERR is the largest independent student-led organization in Washington County. Unlike many programs, we operate without oversight from schools, national networks, or parent organizations. Every initiative we launch is conceived, developed, and executed by students — from strategy to implementation.

This structure gives us the flexibility to respond directly to community needs and the freedom to pursue creative, grassroots solutions. Whether it's organizing a fundraiser, launching a service project, or building new partnerships, our ideas come from the students who live and learn in the communities we serve.

Our Values

  • Collaboration: We thrive on working together, building bridges between students, organizations, and communities.
  • Innovation: Student-driven ideas are at the heart of everything we do, encouraging creative solutions for complex challenges.
  • Sustainability: We focus on long-term impact, ensuring our projects lead to lasting positive change.
